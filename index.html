Daily Ad Operations Schedule

This guide provides a detailed, time-based walkthrough of all tasks to be performed daily to ensure smooth ad distribution.

Morning Routine

The morning is focused on processing the first wave of automated reports and handling new ad placements from the Google Docs.

Task 1: Process Morning Canoe Report

The first task of the day is to process the automated "Promo Missing Ads" report from Canoe to identify and fix ads that have not been received by our affiliates.

How to do it:
  1. Receive and Open: An email from Canoe will arrive around 8:00 AM. Open the two spreadsheet attachments (one for paid, one for unpaid promos).
  2. Analyze the Report:
    • First, sort the entire sheet by **Network (Column B)**.
    • Apply a filter or highlight to all rows where the network is **ABC** or **Disney Channel**. This is your working list for the Charter affiliate. Any other network (FX, Nat Geo) reported as missing for Charter can be ignored.
    • Remove the initial sort and re-sort the sheet by **Comcast (Column N)** and then **Cox (Column P)** to group items for those specific affiliates.
  3. Take Action on Missing Ads:
    • For Standard Ads (ABC, Disney Channel, etc.):
      1. Copy the **Mass ID** of the missing ad.
      2. Search for this Mass ID in Media Hub VOD+.
      3. Confirm the asset is correct and **repitch** it to the specific affiliate (Comcast, Cox, or Charter) listed in the report.
      4. Update your personal tracking list with the repitch date.
    • For FX "MOVE" Assets:
      1. These are identified by an ID starting with "MOVE".
      2. **Do not attempt to repitch these yourself.**
      3. Open a new email to Christopher Freeman.

Task 2: Process Google Docs

After clearing the Canoe report, shift focus to the shared Google Docs to process new ad placements.

How to do it (FX & Nat Geo Workflow):
  1. Open the "FX & Nat Geo Working" Google Doc.
  2. Scan the sheet for any rows with the status **"Awaiting Content"**.
  3. Copy the **ISCI/Provider ID (Column D)**.
  4. In the **Media Dashboard** (not VOD+), search for the ID. If the status is green/completed, proceed.
  5. Check the box next to the completed asset and select **"Generate DAI asset"** from the actions menu.
  6. Navigate to Media Hub VOD+, filter by **NLDD Type: DAI** for today's date, and find the new asset.
  7. Select the asset(s) and click **"Create Planner"**.
  8. Copy the essential planner details (Asset Name, Move Asset ID, Start/End dates) and paste them back into the FX & Nat Geo Working Google Doc, updating the status to **"Complete"**.
How to do it (ABC & Kids Workflow):
  1. Open the "ABC & Kids Working" Google Doc.
  2. Your work begins when an **"X"** appears in the **"Media Service" column (Column N)**.
  3. Copy the **Placing ID** from either the SD (**Column O**) or HD (**Column Q**) column.
  4. Search for this Placing ID in Media Hub VOD+ and verify both SD and HD versions are correct.
  5. Copy the **Mass ID** for both versions from VOD+.
  6. Paste the Mass IDs back into the correct columns in the Google Doc and add the current date to finalize.

Throughout the Day (Ongoing)

Continuously monitor communication channels for ad-hoc issues and troubleshoot as they arise.

  • Monitor the Teams Channel: Keep the `STB VOD Creative - Freeform, ABC & FX/NG` channel open to see real-time issues reported by Ad Managers.
  • Handle GAM Issues: If an Ad Manager reports an asset is not in GAM, perform the standard two-step repitch. If that fails, escalate to Caroline's team.
  • Resolve Missing Media: If an ad is marked with an "X" but is not in Media Hub VOD+, check the file history, contact Media Services, and if necessary, perform a manual purge and republish from Banjo.
  • Manage Duplicates: If a row turns blue (duplicate) in the Google Doc, mark it in red, add a note, and contact the Ad Manager to see if it's an error or an intended update.

Afternoon Routine (Approx. 4:00 PM CDT)

Process the second and final Canoe report of the day.

How to do it:
  1. Receive and Process: An email from Canoe will arrive around 4:00 PM.
  2. Repeat the Morning Process: Follow the exact same analysis and action steps outlined in **Task 1** of the Morning Routine to resolve any newly reported missing ads before the end of the day.

Troubleshooting Guide

Encounter a problem? This section provides a quick guide to common issues and their resolutions. Select an issue from the list below to see the step-by-step fix.

Select an issue to see details.

Resolving GAM Issues

  1. **Initial Repitch:** Repitch the asset to the affiliate in VOD+. Ask the Ad Manager to check again in 30 minutes.
  2. **Check with Canoe:** If the issue persists, email Canoe Operations to confirm they see the asset as delivered.
  3. **Escalate:** If widespread, inform Caroline's team via the `Canoe STB VOD troubleshooting` Teams chat. Contacts: Caroline Bilodeau, Jessica Skold, Torey Beigh. They will open the official ticket.

Resolving Missing Media

  1. **Check Edit History:** Right-click the cell in the Google Doc to see *when* the "X" was added. If it was very recent, wait 30-60 minutes.
  2. **Contact Media Services:** If still missing, ping Sean Smith via Teams or email the group at `datg.media.services@disney.com` to confirm the asset is in a "ready state."
  3. **Purge and Republish:** If Sean confirms it's ready, the asset may be stuck. **Purge it from VOD+ and SDVI**, then find the asset in **Banjo** and click **"Publish"** to resend it.

Handling Duplicate Ads

  1. **Mark as Duplicate:** Highlight the entire row with the designated "duplicate" color and put an "X" in the processing columns.
  2. **Add a Note:** Add a note indicating which line it is a duplicate of (e.g., "Duplicate of line 56").
  3. **Await Instructions:** Contact the **Ad Manager and their Supervisor** (contact info in Google Doc) to clarify if this is a true duplicate or an updated version.

Resolving SD Ad GAM Issue

This is a recurring problem where Standard Definition (SD) ads fail to appear in GAM, even when the HD version is working correctly.

  1. Rule Out User Error:

    Ask the Ad Manager to confirm there are no extra or "phantom" spaces before or after the Placing/Mass ID in GAM and to try typing it manually.

  2. Perform the Two-Step Repitch:

    This specific sequence has a high success rate for this issue:

    • **Step A:** Repitch the SD asset directly to the **affiliate** and wait for completion.
    • **Step B:** After the first delivery is complete, repitch the asset specifically to **GAM**.
  3. Escalation Path:

    For a Single Ad: If the two-step repitch fails, operations team cannot proceed. The Ad Manager must decide whether to create new placing IDs for the ad.

    For Multiple Ads: If the problem affects multiple ads or includes an HD ad, it indicates a larger system issue. Escalate immediately to **Caroline's Team**.
